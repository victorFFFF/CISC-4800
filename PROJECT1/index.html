Information page on Coronavirus

Corona Virus

About Coronavirus

The current pandemic is a Coronavirus known as COVID-19. Coronaviruses are viruses that circulate among animals with some of them also known to infect humans. Bats are considered as natural hosts of these viruses yet several other species of animals are also known to be a source. People who are infected may show little to no symptoms

Where it all started

Patient-zero presumed to be from and in China, Wuhan. Multiple infected patients has been traced back into a animal market in Wuhan. Experts suggest that the virus came from consumption of bats. Bats carry many Coronaviruses. One of them may be COVID-19. The virus has most likely been silently spreading through the streets for at least a few weeks. Ever since, COVID-19 has been spreading rapidly around the globe.

Prevention

  1. ALWAYS wash your hand throughly with soap.
  2. Avoid touching your face as much as possible.
  3. Avoid traveling to another country or going to a tight space and highly populated area if not necessary.
  4. Work from home if possible.
  5. Avoid physical contact with anyone that is sick

Treatement

There is no specific antiviral treatment recommended for COVID-19. People with COVID-19 should receive supportive care to help relieve symptoms. For severe cases, treatment should include care to support vital organ functions. People who think they may have been exposed to COVID-19 should contact their healthcare provider immediately.
